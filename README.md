# AI Arch - AI-Powered Architectural Design Generator

Generate professional architectural designs from simple text prompts. AI_Arch is designed for creative architects who want to leverage AI to quickly conceptualize and visualize architectural ideas without requiring extensive technical knowledge or high-end hardware.

## 🎯 What is AI Arch?

AI_Arch is an intelligent tool that transforms textual descriptions into architectural designs. Simply describe what you want to build—a modern office, a sustainable home, a retail space—and get AI-generated architectural concepts instantly.

**Perfect for:**
- Architects exploring design ideas quickly
- Architects with limited computational resources
- Designers who need rapid concept generation
- Professionals seeking creative inspiration
